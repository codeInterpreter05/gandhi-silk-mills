About Us

Founded in 2008 by Kalpesh Gandhi, Gandhi Silk Mills has established itself as a leading textile manufacturer and supplier in Mumbai.

With over 15 years of experience in the textile industry, we specialize in producing and supplying premium quality fabrics and ready-made garments to wholesalers and retailers across India.

Our commitment to excellence, quality craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the B2B textile market. We offer an extensive range of exclusive fancy fabrics, silk varieties, and ready-made products that meet the highest standards of quality and design.
